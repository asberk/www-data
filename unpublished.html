Documents

  • A proof of Calderòn’s formula

    A short document written [in rough] in preparation for a graded presentation for graduate-level Analysis II with Dr. Almut Burchard. It gives a complete proof of Calderòn’s identity in the general case.

  • Recharacterization of the ROF model for image denoising and TNV multiscale image decomposition

    This paper provides the necessary background to understand selected methods in nonlinear image processing. In particular, this paper focuses on a special implementation of the Osher-Rudin model for image de-noising over the Besov space B^1_1(L^1); the computation of the minimizer for this functional; and the application of this model to a TNV-type multiscale image decomposition.

    This document contains preliminary work relevant to the summer project I completed as a requisite of the M.Sc. program (see below).

  • On multiscale analysis and PDE methods on graphs in image processing

    A research project completed with Dr. Adrian Nachman as my supervisor. Focuses on the implementation and structure of fast nonlinear methods for data analysis and image processing.
